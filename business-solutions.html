What is the $PIZZA Platform?

The $PIZZA Platform is a unified Solana-based payment system optimized for exactly 10 business partners. We operate on a shared platform vault model with fixed $15 USDC transactions, distinguishing between NCN (Non-Crypto Native) and CN (Crypto Native) businesses with different fee structures and settlement preferences.

Fixed $15 USDC transactions - Every transaction is exactly $15 USDC (no variable amounts), creating predictable revenue streams and simplified accounting for your business.

Our platform automatically handles currency conversion, fee processing, and customer rewards distribution in real-time, allowing you to focus on what you do best - running your business.

How It Works

1. Customer Payment Flow

Point of Sale: Customers pay $15 USDC via Solana Pay QR scan ($0.00025/transfer cost).

Atomic Swap: $PIZZA SPL payments are automatically swapped to USDC via Jupiter (0.75% customer loss), ensuring merchant stability.

2. Automatic Fee Processing

3. Customer Rewards (Automatic)

Every customer automatically receives 0.3 $PIZZA SPL per transaction ($0.30 perceived value, $0.15 actual cost). This is funded by the platform vault, requiring zero effort from you while building incredible customer loyalty.
